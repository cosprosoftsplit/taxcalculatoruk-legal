Tracking Transparency

If you allow tracking through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt, Google may use the device advertising identifier to show more relevant ads. If you decline, the free version may still show non-personalized ads.

This app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the app, please contact us.
